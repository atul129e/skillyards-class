Classic Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch. Simple ingredients, quick
instruction, and guaranteed comfort.

Stack of pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoon melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powser, baking soda,
    and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ongredients and gently mix
    until combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles from, then flip
    and cook until golden.
